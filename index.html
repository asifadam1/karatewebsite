About Us

Shorai Conflict and Prevention was established by Sensei Imran Majid with the authority of Sensei Joe Tierney around a decade ago. SCP is Voluntary organisation managed and run by trainer Imran Majid who is qualified himself to the highest calibre, saw a passion to link the community buildings into a more than just a place of worship, but to provide opportunities beyond the Madrasa curriculum. Supported by the Sport Health and Inclusion Services and the dedicated support by the volunteers, the Club has seen itself grow and become more sustainable with children having their own uniform to competing in Tournaments and bringing back the winning trophies
For further information contact Sensei Imran Majid on 07713266507 - SCP Karate

Licences

In order to meet legal requirements, your child must have a valid license at all times. If the license has expired, they are not covered by insurance and therefore it is illegal for them to train or grade at the club. If it has expired, it will need to be renewed
